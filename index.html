Cara Penggunaan Regular & Irregular Verbs

Regular Verbs

Gunakan akhiran -ed atau -d untuk bentuk Past Tense (V2) dan Past Participle (V3).

  • Simple Past: I played football yesterday.
  • Present Perfect: She has studied hard.

Ingat aturan ejaan: study → studied, stop → stopped.

Irregular Verbs

Bentuk V2 dan V3-nya unik (tidak pakai -ed). Harus dihafal per kata.

  • Simple Past: He went to the market.
  • Present Perfect: They have gone abroad.

Beberapa irregular punya pola tertentu (mis. sing-sang-sung).

Tips Cepat

  • Pola vokal: sing → sang → sung, drink → drank → drunk, begin → began → begun.
  • Sama semua: put → put → put, cut → cut → cut, hit → hit → hit.
  • V2 = V3: build → built → built, send → sent → sent.
  • Hafal kelompok: misalnya family keep-kept-kept, sleep-slept-slept.
  • Practice kalimat: buat 1 kalimat tiap bentuk → I write, I wrote, I have written.

Gunakan pola + practice biar lebih gampang diingat daripada ngafalin 1 per 1.
